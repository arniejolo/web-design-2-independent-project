The three Marys of m³

Arnie Jolo

Owner/Artistic Director/Graphic Designer

Arnie Jolo is a graphic designer with a background in psychology. She has always been interested in creative realms especially illustration, painting and fashion design. She has completed 2 years studying design and is a student member of the RGD. Her current experience at Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada helps elevate knowledge in corporate settings of graphic design.

Khryslyne Delacruz

Artistic Consultant/Architect/Designer

Khryslyne Delacruz is an artistic consultant who uses her eye for design to provide feedback to clients and designers within the company. She holds a certificate in Design Studies and is an Architectural Technician who provides a wide range of knowledge to all kinds of designers.

Jaimee Jolo

Client Relations/Administration

Jaimee Jolo is our client relations specialist who has extensive experience in coordination and scheduling and deals with all aspects of admin in the company. Jaimee holds a Biology degree with a minor in Music and has skillsets to make clients to feel comfortable and able to participate and communicate freely with our team.
